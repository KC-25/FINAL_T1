------- Cyber Equinox -------

Welcome detective, the year is 5050 and you work in the city of Silvandraxia. The town is more alive than ever as the cyber equinox approaches, but there have been troubling events leading up to the party. An unknown person has been killing off members of the scientific community and stealing thier technology, but why? You look forward to Professor Mumm's reveal of his mysterious eco chip but worry if he'll be next. It is rumored to be able to save the world, and if you can solve the case it can do just that.
